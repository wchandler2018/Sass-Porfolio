My future: My Passion for coding

When I left the military it was hard for me to find a passion for anything else. I originally had my heart set on going to Boston College Law School(that didn't go to well) but I quickly realize that law was not passion. One day my neighbor, a web developer for a small marketing company showed what he does for work and it was love and first site. I quickly began to read books, go to local programming meetups, I watched 100s of hours YouTube videos & when I finally made my first website while not the prettiest one ever but I couldn't wait to show my friends and family what I had done. Programming has made me excited to learn again, I get up in the morning and can't decide what to work on, a React project, a Python project or maybe a really cool website. I have finally the same passion that I had being a Soldier and I am so excited and optomistic to see what the future will bring.
